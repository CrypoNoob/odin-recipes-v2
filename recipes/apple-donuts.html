Apple donuts

Ingredients for two servings (8 pieces): 2 medium apples 3 tablespoons maple syrup A quarter teaspoon of cinnamon egg 5 tablespoons white cheese A tablespoon of peanut butter Half a teaspoon of baking powder How to prepare for the apple base: Slice a medium sweet apple not peeled into 4 slices about 1 cm thick, and remove the seeds so that a hole remains in the center. Put the maple and cinnamon in a pan and fry over low heat until slightly thickened. Add the apple slices and fry until caramelized. Put them on a cooling plate at room temperature. How to prepare for the donut batter: Mix the egg, white cheese, peanut butter and baking powder. Dip the apples in the batter with a fork and shake off excess. Fry in a pan (with oil spray) until golden on both sides. Using a spoon, open the hole if it is clogged and dust with a little powdered sugar. enjoy your meal
