Apple donuts

Description

A simple yet delicious recipe for anyone who's looking for a healthy desert!

Ingredients

(for two servings or 8 pieces)

  • 2 medium apples
  • 3 tablespoons maple syrup
  • A quarter teaspoon of cinnamon
  • An Egg
  • 1 tablespoon of white cheese 5% fat
  • A tablespoon of peanut butter
  • Half a teaspoon of baking powder

Steps

  1. The Apple Base
    1. Slice a medium sweet apple not peeled into 4 slices about 1 cm thick, and remove the seeds so that a hole remains in the center.
    2. Put the maple and cinnamon in a pan and fry over low heat until slightly thickened.
    3. Add the apple slices and fry until caramelized.
    4. Put them on a cooling plate at room temperature.
  2. The Donut Batter
    1. Mix the egg, white cheese, peanut butter and baking powder.
    2. Dip the apples in the batter with a fork and shake off excess.
    3. Fry in a pan (with oil spray) until golden on both sides.
    4. Using a spoon, open the hole if it is clogged and dust with a little powdered sugar.
  3. Enjoy Your Meal
